Introduction

At St. Paul American School Hanoi, we understand the importance of providing students with nutritious and diverse meal options. Our lunch program is designed to cater to various tastes while ensuring balanced nutrition.

Daily Menu Options

Each day, our canteen offers two main choices:

  • Asian Cuisine: Traditional dishes reflecting the rich flavors of the region.
  • Western Cuisine: Familiar favorites from Western culinary traditions.

In addition to these main courses, students and staff have access to a full-service salad bar, promoting healthy eating habits.

Monthly Menu Planning

We believe in transparency and collaboration with parents. A carefully planned menu is sent to parents one month in advance. This allows families to:

  • Review upcoming meal options.
  • Select daily lunch preferences using our online canteen system.

For elementary students, parents assist in making meal choices. Secondary students have the autonomy to log in and select their meals independently.

Online Canteen System

Our user-friendly online system streamlines the meal selection process:

  • Access: Log in using provided credentials.
  • Review: Browse the monthly menu.
  • Select: Choose preferred meals for each day.
  • Submit: Confirm selections to ensure accurate meal preparation.

This system ensures that every student receives meals tailored to their preferences and dietary needs.

Nutritional Balance

We prioritize the health and well-being of our students. Our menus are crafted to provide:

  • Balanced Nutrition: Incorporating essential food groups.
  • Variety: Offering different dishes to prevent monotony.
  • Quality Ingredients: Sourcing fresh and wholesome produce.

By doing so, we aim to support the physical and cognitive development of our students.

Conclusion

St. Paul American School Hanoi is committed to offering a lunch program that meets the diverse tastes and nutritional needs of our students. Through careful planning and collaboration with parents, we ensure that every meal contributes positively to the student experience.
